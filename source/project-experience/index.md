### Research Assistant — Rhythms of Plant VOCs Induced by *Spodoptera exigua*

**Research Internship (CSIC)** | January – April 2026 | Supervisor: Dr. Xoaquín Moreira

Served as a research assistant on a project investigating the circadian rhythms of plant volatile organic compound (VOC) emission induced by herbivory from *Spodoptera exigua* (beet armyworm), and the corresponding response rhythms in neighboring receiver plants. Responsibilities included experimental setup, plant growth and herbivore colony management, VOC collection and analysis assistance, and data recording. This work contributed to understanding plant-plant communication via induced volatiles and their temporal dynamics, with implications for tritrophic interactions and chemical ecology.

*在CSIC（西班牙国家研究委员会）担任科研助理，研究甜菜夜蛾取食诱导植物挥发性有机物（VOC）的昼夜节律及受体植物的响应规律，涉及化学生态学与植物间通讯研究。*

## Research Projects & Innovation Programs 科研项目 / 大创

### China National Innovation & Entrepreneurship Training Program (National-level)

**Impact of Climate Change on Phenology of Northeastern Forests** | Team Member | May 2022 – May 2024

As a team member in this national-level innovation program, contributed to a two-year research project examining how climate change affects the phenological patterns of forests in northeastern China. Applied remote sensing technologies and GIS (ArcGIS) to analyze satellite imagery and spatial data, tracking vegetation indices across different forest types. The project integrated field survey data with remote sensing analysis to identify trends in bud burst, leaf senescence, and other phenological indicators under climate variability scenarios.

*国家级大学生创新创业训练计划，研究气候变化对东北森林物候的影响，运用遥感技术及GIS（ArcGIS）对植被指数和物候参数进行时空分析。*
