🦞 Lobster Recommendation Guide | 龙虾推荐指南

As a seafood lover, lobster holds a special place in my heart. Whether you are trying it for the first time or looking for your next great lobster experience, this guide covers the most popular types and best ways to enjoy them.

作为一个海鲜爱好者，龙虾在我心中有着特别的地位。无论你是第一次尝试，还是寻找下一次美好的龙虾体验，这篇指南涵盖了最受欢迎的龙虾种类和最佳的享用方式。

🌊 Types of Lobster | 龙虾种类

1. Boston Lobster (American Lobster) | 波士顿龙虾

The classic cold-water lobster from the North Atlantic. Known for its large claws and sweet, tender meat. Best enjoyed steamed or boiled with drawn butter.
来自北大西洋的经典冷水龙虾，以其大螯和鲜甜嫩滑的肉质著称。最适合清蒸或水煮后搭配黄油食用。

  • Best season: Late summer to early autumn | 最佳季节：夏末至初秋
  • Recommended preparation: Steamed whole, Lobster roll | 推荐做法：整只清蒸、龙虾卷
  • Flavor profile: Sweet, delicate, slightly briny | 口感：鲜甜、细腻、略带海水味

2. Spiny Lobster (Rock Lobster) | 龙虾 / 锦绣龙虾

Found in warmer waters, these lobsters have no claws but their tails are packed with meat. Extremely popular in Chinese cuisine, often grilled with garlic or prepared in a hot pot.
生活在温暖水域，没有螯足，但尾部肉质丰满。在中国菜中极受欢迎，常见做法有蒜蓉焗烤或火锅。

  • Best season: Year-round | 最佳季节：全年
  • Recommended preparation: Garlic butter baked, Hot pot, Sashimi | 推荐做法：蒜蓉焗、火锅、刺身
  • Flavor profile: Rich, firm, slightly sweet | 口感：浓郁、紧实、微甜

3. Slipper Lobster | 扇虾 / 琵琶虾

A flat-bodied lobster with a distinctive shape. Its meat is incredibly sweet and is considered a delicacy in many Asian cuisines.
身体扁平、形态独特的龙虾。肉质极为鲜甜，在许多亚洲菜系中被视为珍馐。

  • Recommended preparation: Steamed, Grilled with cheese | 推荐做法：清蒸、芝士焗
  • Flavor profile: Very sweet, delicate | 口感：极甜、细腻

🍽️ Top Cooking Methods | 最佳烹饪方式

  1. Steamed (清蒸) — Preserves the natural sweetness. Best for fresh, high-quality lobsters. 保留天然鲜味，最适合新鲜优质的龙虾。
  2. Garlic Butter Baked (蒜蓉焗) — The garlic and butter enhance the richness of the meat. A crowd favorite. 蒜蓉和黄油提升肉质的鲜美，深受大众喜爱。
  3. Thermidor (忌廮龙虾) — A French classic: lobster meat with a creamy wine and cheese sauce, then grilled. 法式经典：龙虾肉搭配奶油白酒芝士酱，再烤至金黄。
  4. Lobster Roll — Chunks of lobster meat with mayo, served in a toasted bun. A New England staple. 龙虾肉拌蛋黄酱，夹入烤面包中，新英格兰经典小吃。
  5. Hot Pot (火锅) — Drop it live into a simmering broth for a fresh and fun dining experience. 将活龙虾放入滚烫的火锅汤底，鲜味十足。

💡 Buying Tips | 购买贴士

  • Always buy live lobsters when possible — freshness is everything. 尽量购买活龙虾，新鲜度决定一切。
  • Look for lobsters that are active and responsive. 选择活跃有反应的龙虾。
  • Avoid lobsters with missing claws or legs — they may have been stressed or kept too long. 避免缺螯或缺腿的龙虾，可能已经过度应激或久放。
  • For frozen tails, look for IQF (Individually Quick Frozen) products without added water or phosphates. 冷冻虾尾请选择IQF（单体速冻）产品，避免添加水分或磷酸盐。
