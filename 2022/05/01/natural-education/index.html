Natural Education Projects | 自然教育实践

Project Leader, National Innovation & Entrepreneurship Training Program (2022.5 – 2023.5)
项目负责人

Overview

I have been deeply involved in natural education through China’s National Innovation & Entrepreneurship Training Program, designing educational tools, building community platforms, and publishing research. My work has been recognized at the national level, with awards, patents, and publications in both education and design.

Project 1: “Nature Story” — Exploring Natural Education

Role: Project Leader | Level: National-level Innovation & Entrepreneurship Training Program | Duration: May 2022 – May 2023

Key Achievements

🌱 WeChat Public Platform — “物语自然 Nature Story”

  • Founded and operated the official WeChat account “Nature Story” (物语自然Nature Story)
  • Published educational content on local flora, fauna, and ecological concepts
  • Total views exceeded 1,500 — demonstrating strong community engagement

💧 “Wu Water Plan” (吾水计划) — Rural Water Education

  • Selected for the 10th cohort of the national “Wu Water Plan” rural water education volunteer program
  • Led a team to Qiqihar, Heilongjiang to conduct hands-on water science education for local students
  • Our work was covered by local news media, highlighting the program’s community impact

🐯 Wildlife Park Natural Education Content

  • Authored educational course materials for the Northeast Tiger and Forest Park (东北虎林园) natural education online program
  • Content covered tiger ecology, forest ecosystems, and human-wildlife coexistence

  • Submitted to China Forestry Education (《中国林业教育》)

Project 2: Climate Change Impact on Forest Phenology

Role: Team Member | Level: National-level Innovation & Entrepreneurship Training Program | Duration: May 2022 – May 2024

Contributions

  • Data processing — Collected and processed multi-scale meteorological data (temperature, precipitation) from 2000–2019, rasterized and clipped to research areas
  • NDVI analysis — Used R package phenofit for NDVI time series curve fitting and significance analysis
  • Remote sensing tools — Applied Google Earth Engine, ArcGIS, and ENVI for remote sensing data processing and visualization
  • Paper writing — Contributed to the paper on climate change impacts on Northeast China forest phenology

Project 3: Data Visualization for Sustainable Marine Economy

Role: Team Leader | Duration: July 2022

Achievements

  • Led a team investigating the business operations of major fisheries enterprises
  • Collected data through corporate databases (企查查) and field visits with local fishermen
  • Published research paper “Analysis of Chinese Turbot Industry Development and Existing Problems” in Beautiful China (《美丽中国》) journal

Reflection

Natural education became the bridge between my academic interests and my passion for public engagement. Through designing educational tools, leading field programs, and publishing research, I learned that effective science communication requires not just knowledge but creativity and empathy. The rainfall simulator patent exemplifies this — transforming a complex hydrological concept into a tangible classroom experience. These experiences shaped my commitment to making science accessible and actionable, a philosophy I carry into my ecological research career.
