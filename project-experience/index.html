Project Experience

Project Experience | 项目经历

Field Study & Internships 野外实习 / 实地考察

Ulungur Lake National Wetland Park Management Bureau — Bird Rehabilitation & Breeding Center

Bird Detection & Identification | Jun – Jul 2023, Fuhai County, Xinjiang

Stationed at the Bird Rehabilitation & Breeding Center of Ulungur Lake National Wetland Park, participated in systematic wild bird detection and species identification across the lake wetland. Assisted in daily care and rehabilitation of injured birds, including feeding management, health monitoring, and pre-release assessment. Conducted environmental conservation patrols and compiled species observation data for submission to local regulatory authorities. Gained hands-on experience in wetland bird ecology and conservation management of an arid-zone inland lake ecosystem.

乌伦古湖国家湿地公园管理局（福海县）鸟类救助繁育中心，从事鸟类检测与识别工作。参与野生鸟类日常照料与救助、环境保护巡查及相关数据整理上报工作。

Comprehensive Production Internship in Wildlife Domestication & Breeding

Wildlife Breeding Internship | May 2022, Harbin

Participated in a comprehensive production internship covering multiple wildlife-related facilities around Harbin. Visits included a police dog base, equestrian center, wildlife park, and deer park. Key learning areas included breeding facility planning and management, enclosure design principles, animal feeding techniques, and feed processing & formulation methods. This internship provided practical insights into captive wildlife management and the operational aspects of breeding centers for various species.

野生动物驯养繁殖综合生产实习，涵盖警犬基地、马术中心、野生动物园及鹿场参观，学习繁殖场规划、围栏设计、饲养技术及饲料配制。

Field Study on Human-Wildlife Conflict in East Africa, Kenya

Field Investigation | January 2026, Kenya, East Africa

Conducted a field study in Kenya focusing on the patterns, causes, and mitigation strategies of human-wildlife conflict (HWC) at the interface between local communities and wildlife habitats. Participated in field surveys, community interviews, and observations of local conservation management practices. Engaged with wildlife rangers, local communities, and conservation organizations to understand conflict dynamics involving elephants, large carnivores, and other wildlife. This experience deepened understanding of community-based conservation, coexistence strategies, and the socio-ecological challenges of wildlife management in sub-Saharan Africa.

赴肯尼亚开展东非人兽冲突野外考察，参与社区调查与访谈，了解大象、大型食肉动物等野生动物与人类社区的冲突模式及缓解策略，接触社区保护实践与共存管理经验。

📷 [Photo placeholder: Field survey in Kenya]

Research Assistant — Rhythms of Plant VOCs Induced by Spodoptera exigua

Research Internship (CSIC) | January – April 2026 | Supervisor: Dr. Xoaquín Moreira

Served as a research assistant on a project investigating the circadian rhythms of plant volatile organic compound (VOC) emission induced by herbivory from Spodoptera exigua (beet armyworm), and the corresponding response rhythms in neighboring receiver plants. Responsibilities included experimental setup, plant growth and herbivore colony management, VOC collection and analysis assistance, and data recording. This work contributed to understanding plant-plant communication via induced volatiles and their temporal dynamics, with implications for tritrophic interactions and chemical ecology.

在CSIC（西班牙国家研究委员会）担任科研助理，研究甜菜夜蛾取食诱导植物挥发性有机物（VOC）的昼夜节律及受体植物的响应规律，涉及化学生态学与植物间通讯研究。

Research Projects & Innovation Programs 科研项目 / 大创

China National Innovation & Entrepreneurship Training Program (National-level)

Impact of Climate Change on Phenology of Northeastern Forests | Team Member | May 2022 – May 2024

As a team member in this national-level innovation program, contributed to a two-year research project examining how climate change affects the phenological patterns (timing of seasonal biological events) of forests in northeastern China. Applied remote sensing technologies and GIS (ArcGIS) to analyze satellite imagery and spatial data, tracking vegetation indices across different forest types. The project integrated field survey data with remote sensing analysis to identify trends in bud burst, leaf senescence, and other phenological indicators under climate variability scenarios.

国家级大学生创新创业训练计划，研究气候变化对东北森林物候的影响，运用遥感技术及GIS（ArcGIS）对植被指数和物候参数进行时空分析。

China National Innovation & Entrepreneurship Training Program (University-level)

"Nature Story — Exploring the Path of Nature Education" | Team Lead | May 2022 – May 2023

Led a university-level innovation program exploring models and pathways for nature education in China. The project involved sector surveys of existing nature education programs, organizing practice activities including participation in the MyH2O water safety education initiative, designing a utility-model (a rainfall simulator for nature education classrooms, granted patent CN217880630U), and competing in innovation competitions. As team lead, coordinated multi-stage deliverables and guided teammates through research design, fieldwork, and presentation preparation.

校级大创，以自然教育路径探索为主题，开展行业调研、实践活动（含MyH2O项目），完成实用新型专利设计（循环降雨模拟器，专利号CN217880630U）并参加创新创业大赛。

📷 [Photo placeholder: Nature education activities / rainfall simulator prototype]

Public-service Practice: Data Visualization for Sustainable Marine Economy

Team Lead | June 2022, Haikou

Led a public-service practice project focused on data visualization for China's sustainable marine economy. Analyzed datasets related to China's fisheries industry and the turbot aquaculture sector, creating visual representations to communicate trends and policy implications. The team's analysis also resulted in a co-authored publication on turbot industry development and challenges in China. This project bridged data science skills with marine resource management policy awareness.

带领团队开展可持续海洋经济数据可视化实践项目，分析中国渔业及大菱鲆产业数据，并合作发表相关论文。

Online Zoology Research Topic: Climate & Environmental Crisis on Animal Survival

Team Lead | May – July 2023 | Guided by Prof. Andre (Cornell University)

Led an online research group studying the impacts of climate change and environmental crisis on animal survival, reproduction, and conservation strategies. Under the guidance of Prof. Andre from Cornell University, the team explored biological responses to climate change including species range shifts, phenological mismatches, behavioral adaptations, and extinction risks. The project culminated in a research report and a peer-reviewed publication in Highlights in Science, Engineering and Technology.

担任小组负责人，在美国康奈尔大学Andre教授指导下，研究气候与环境危机对动物生存的影响及保护策略，成果发表于《Highlights in Science, Engineering and Technology》。

Master's Thesis Research — Urban Rat Project

Research Team Member | May 2025 – present | University of Helsinki
Supervisors: Dr. Tuomas Aivelo & Dr. Viktor Zöldi

Conducting master's thesis research as part of the Urban Rat Project at the University of Helsinki, which investigates urban rat behavior, ecology, and population dynamics. Research focuses on predator/non-predator odor response experiments to study anti-predator behaviors in urban brown rats (Rattus norvegicus). Also supports team efforts in field surveys and sampling. This research contributes to understanding how urban wildlife adapt behaviorally to human-dominated environments and informs urban pest management strategies.

赫尔辛基大学硕士论文研究，属城市大鼠项目，研究褐家鼠对天敌/非天敌气味的行为反应，同时参与野外种群调查与采样工作。导师：Tuomas Aivelo博士 & Viktor Zöldi博士。

NEFU Wetland Conservation Association

President | 2022 – 2023 | Northeast Forestry University

Served as president of the Northeast Forestry University Wetland Conservation Association, leading a student-driven organization dedicated to wetland biodiversity education and conservation advocacy. Key activities during tenure included organizing the writing of species Red List assessments for Xingkai Lake, leading the editing and publication of a campus bird and plant guidebook, conducting outreach activities on the Wetland Protection Law, and facilitating academic and cultural exchanges with RUDN University (Russia).
